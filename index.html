Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using Aasaan.

Is Aasaan free?

Yes. End users and providers can use Aasaan for free. Providers can upgrade to Pro or Pro Plus for added benefits.

What do I need to sign up?

A smartphone and your mobile number. Login and registration are via OTP — no passwords required.

Which languages are supported?

Hindi, English, and regional Indian languages, with instant switching from the language selection screen.

What permissions does the app need?

Location (to match nearby jobs), Phone (to call after acceptance), OTP read (for faster login), and optional Contacts on Android for identifying potential providers. You can control these anytime.

Can I switch between End User and Service Provider?

Yes. Switch roles from your profile. If you’re a provider, set your service, location, and radius to start receiving jobs.

Is my data secure?

We store mobile numbers in encrypted form and follow privacy‑first practices.
